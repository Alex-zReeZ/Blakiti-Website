Accueil

Our commitment to environmental preservation is reflected in the design of our product. We have taken concrete steps to ensure nature is respected at every stage of its creation. As part of our ecological initiative, we are proud to announce that for every batch of 10 products purchased, a tree will be planted.

All our creations are meticulously crafted with the utmost care. Each product is hand-engraved with meticulous precision, then sanded with absolute rigor, and finally carefully cleaned. We pay special attention to the painting phase, which is done with exclusive expertise. Each step of this process ensures an impeccably clean and incredibly sharp final result. Once this craftsmanship is complete, our products are ready to be shipped directly to your address, ensuring you receive a finished and sophisticated piece that testifies to our commitment to exceptional quality.

The video we are sharing illustrates the process of making our products. You will see step by step how our items take shape, from the selection of materials to the final realization.
